Dumplings

Have you ever wanted to learn how to cook authentic dumplings?

Dumplings have been an asian food staple since the early eighteenth century and despite its traditional roots, it makes its way into our eastern kitchen.

A dumpling is a meat stuffed dough thats either steamed or boiled. the picture above is an example of steamed dumplings

You'll need...

  • Dumpling dough
  • Ground beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the filling by taking your ground beef and placing it into a bowl.
  2. Add the soy sauce, salt, and pepper into the bowl with the beef.
  3. Knead the beef.
  4. Take out the wrappers and place a small ball of filling onto the center of the wrapper.
  5. Wrap in anyway you like.
  6. Boil directly into pot or steam in a basket.
  7. After five to eight minutes take out and let cool.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
